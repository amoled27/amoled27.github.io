Leadership Team

The Team

We have a governance structure in place that is made up of executive and non-executive directors. The governance structure is made up of individuals with varied professional and business backgrounds

Mr. Kagisho Bapela

CEO CA(SA)

Kagisho is a qualified CA with extensive experience in deal making, gained from DFIs, as well as audit and governance. He is responsible for the day-to-day management of group companies as well as governance structures.

Ms. Thandy Mokgobu

CFO

Thandy has extensive knowledge and experience in Internal Auditing, Corporate Governance as well as Financial and Risk Management. She is responsible for internal control, budgeting and reporting for the entire group.

Mr. Kingsley Duba

Executive Chairman

Kingsley is the founder and Chairman of Mokoena Holdings. Kingsley was previously a lecturer of strategic planning & management at University of the North’s Graduate School of Leadership. Kingsley also served on the advisory committee of the MBA programme at the University of the North. He also previously served as a Board Member and Chairperson of the Finance committee at Polokwane International Airport, Small Enterprise Development Agency (SEDA), Limpopo Road Agency and Gauteng Enterprise Propeller.

Kingsley currently chairs the Valve and Actuators Manufacturing Cluster of South Africa (VAMCOSA) whilst he continues to grow Mokoena Holdings and its subsidiaries within South Africa, the African continent at large and globally.

Pierre de la Guerre

General Manager (PSC Integrity)

Pierre is the General Manager of PSC Integrity and started his inspection career in 2008. In 2015 he obtained a Competent Person Steam Generator certification from the SAIW. In that same year, he became an API 510 Certified Pressure Vessel Inspector from the American Petroleum Institute. Following this, Pierre joined PSC in 2016 and was awarded an IPE certification in 2017. His most recent accreditation is the API 580 Risk based inspection certification from the American Petroleum Institute in 2018.

Theo Molamu

General Manager (Paltech)

Theo Molamu is the General Manager at Paltech and has extensive experience in executing strategies, process & systems management plans and aligning schedules for companies in developing investment plans. Over the past 20 years, Theo has implemented turn around strategies and transformation initiatives in a variety of industries, including: steel manufacturing, land systems (military equipment), Telecoms, insurance, Aviation, Power and manufacturing. He is responsible for the daily operations at Paltech and implementation of strategic imperatives.

Dr Andre Botha

General Manager (Groupline)

Dr Andre is the General Manager at Groupline and has more than 20 years of Business Consulting experience at an executive level. His wide range of expertise includes strategic management and leadership within large organisations. Dr Andre has experience driving and communicating sales strategies and internal transformation initiatives, all while managing projects that require strategic analysis, restructuring and change management.
