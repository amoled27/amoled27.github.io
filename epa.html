EPA

ePa is a powerful communication tool that can use any messaging platform to interact with the user, fetch the required information queried by the user from any application and provide on the preferred platform.

  • Gather information form the web
  • Role based access to data
  • Can communicate to the IoT devices and control them remotely
  • Integrated with Amazon Alexa and Google Home.
