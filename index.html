ABOUT SHANIA

Shania is a Goan DJ and Music producer

Greetings from the land that's all about beaches and parties, Goa! I'm Shania Tashya, a Disc Jockey based in India. I'm 17 and am currently India's youngest female DJ. I was merely 12 when i figured that i wanted to turn my passion for music into a career and at 15 with heaps and loads of support from home, i started off by playing at some local venues here in Goa and deeply steeped into the nightlife scenes and moved onto playing at some of India's finest clubs.I've shared the console with some luminaries in the music industry- Martin Garrix , Jason Derulo , Wiz Khalifa , Henry Fong , Nucleya , Marnik , Zaeden , Teri Miko and many more. I'm mainly into Moombahton and House music. Also, I always look forward to playing Tropical House music and some groovy tunes for sundowners! It is when I am mixing that I’m at ease, yet hyped up on adrenaline and the desire to grow, to get better and to get the crowd involved and moving with my sets. I see a great potential in the music industry and am working towards taking over the scenes with a storm!
