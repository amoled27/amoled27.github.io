Overview

Although young, the business is dedicated to the very best of service quality and value for money solutions for all its customers.

Badiredi Travel cc is a 100% Black Women owned/managed Travel Agency. It is a Customer Focused entity born out of the need to provide innovative and efficient services in the Limpopo Province for the discerning customer, both in the Private and Public Sectors.

Nomthandazo Nomthi Ximba

Founder & Manager

Nomthandazo “Nomthi” Ximba the founder and manager of the business has finally brought a unique, fresh solution to the customer in the Limpopo Province that is bent on using the very best agents/partners when it comes to business, leisure and recreational travel, which is, the fastest developing Provinces in South Africa. Her endeavour to start the business is inspired by the many accolades she has received from a myriad of satisfied customers.
