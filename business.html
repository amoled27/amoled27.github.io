Our Business

Manufacturing Services

Mokoena Holdings owns Paltech, a valve manufacturing company based in Centurion, specialising in the design, manufacturing, refurbishment and maintenance of low, medium, high pressure valves, actuators and allied equipment for the industrial process control industry.

Major clients: Eskom, SGB Smit-Power, Actom, Brilor, Inbal, Anglo-America Ashanti

For information: www.paltech.co.za

Engineering Services

Mokoena Holdings engineering services center around pressure testing, inspection and Non-Destructive Testing of pressure equipment, pressure vessels, steel structures and tanks. These services are carried out through PSC Integrity and Integrity NDT, entities that are both wholly owned by Mokoena Holdings. PSC Integrity is a SANAS Approved Inspection Authority (AIA) for in-service inspection and fabrication. Integrity NDT performs specialist non-destructive testing on welding, piping or any structure where radiographic testing is required

Major clients: Eskom power stations: Kusile, Lethabo, Medupi, Hendrina, Tongaat Hullet

Industrial Products

The latest entrant to the Mokoena Holdings stable is Groupline - an industrial lining solutions provider. Groupline specialises in specification, selection and installation of lining products to solve a variety of materials handling, flow and wear problems mainly in the mining industry.

Major clients: Eskom power stations: Kusile, Lethabo, Medupi, Hendrina, Tongaat Hullet Weir Minerals Africa, Medupi Power Station, Moatise Coal Mine, Grootgeluk Expansion, Khumani Whims and Expansion Projects, Eskom: Various Maintenance contracts, South 32, SA Coal Anglo, Sasol, Stefmet Technologies

For more information: www.groupline.co.za

Special Projects

The special projects Mokoena Holdings undertakes, tap into the core values at the heart of the organisation. These are projects that aim to improve the status quo and uplift communities anywhere in the world. One such project is the provision of humanitarian aid to South Sudan in the form of food aid, in partnership with the Department of International Relations and Cooperation.

The responsibility to source products, map and process logistics to ensure efficient delivery of product to South Sudan was incumbent on Mokoena Holdings. Our aim is to be involved in similar projects on an on-going basis.
