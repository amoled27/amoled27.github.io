About Us

Level 1 BBBEE

Investment holdings company

Mokoena Holdings (Pty) Ltd is a Level 1 BBBEE black-owned and managed investment holdings company. We believe in a hands-on approach to investing where we can exact influence to enable superior earnings and long-term dividend growth.

Though the majority of our investment activities are primarily within the South African market, we have established strategic partnerships on the continent and globally in the sectors within which are investment interests lie.

Our interests

Our interests can be categorised into 3 sectors:
  • Manufacturing & Engineering Services
  • Industrial Products Service Provider
  • Special Projects

Investment strategy

Our investment strategy focuses on creating a vertically integrated manufacturing and engineering services portfolio of complementary products and services. We target small to medium-sized businesses with the technical know, equipment, human capital, certifications and capacity to expand.
