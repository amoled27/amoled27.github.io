About Us

Hamonate Consulting specialises in the provision of experienced consultants in the SAP and Microsoft software suites.

Hamonate Consulting, part of the Hamonate Group of Companies, is a 100% black owned company and was established in 2005 by a group of IT and Financial professionals who have been in industry for a number of years.

As a black-owned and managed company, one of our key objectives is the advancement of designated groups in the workplace. We have developed Hamonate’s Economic Empowerment Philosophy on the following fundamental objectives:

Objectives

  •     To build an Information Business Solutions Company that reflects the demographics of South Africa;
  •     To ensure the advancement of women in a male-dominated sector;
  •     To build capacity by focusing on training and the transfer of skills

Core Business Focus

  •    Outsourcing,
  •    Recruitment/Staffing Solutions,
  •    Business Consulting using the SAP and Microsoft software suite of products, and
  •    Implementation, development, maintenance/ support and education service offerings to support these solutions.
