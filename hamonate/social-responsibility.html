Social Responsibility

Hamonate's corporate social responsibility program, which supports community-based projects, includes the provision of support, funding, supply of computer equipment, and voluntary services of staff.

Among the projects supported by Hamonate are the following:

Computer equipment was donated to a few churches in Soweto to assist with the consolidation of their administration, and installations and training were done by our staff.
Students with potential IT skills are sourced from universities, colleges and technikons to receive hands-on training with Hamonate. Long-term opportunities are offered to those who qualify.
One of the key focus areas is the company’s drive to identify, develop and promote the successof black-owned business that it is in partnership with. These partners are provided with training and guidance to help them deliver IT solutions and grow their businesses.
Training and development – A wide range of programs and initiatives designed to empower individuals to realise their personal potential and fulfill their career aspirations are used by Hamonate, to foster the need to develop outstanding and dedicated people who are part of a successful team.
Thuthukane Primary School based in Finetown is continuously assisted on IT issues.
